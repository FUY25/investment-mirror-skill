# Ray Dalio

## Source-Backed Biography

Ray Dalio founded Bridgewater Associates and is associated with macro frameworks, risk parity, and principles-based process.

## Investment Style

Macro and portfolio-process investing that emphasizes economic regimes, diversification, systematic principles, and scenario thinking.

## Notable Results And Track-Record Context

Bridgewater research and Dalio's public writing are useful for macro-regime and process language, not for personalized allocation advice.

This profile intentionally avoids unsupported annualized-return claims, league-table rankings, and any implication that the master endorses a current security.

## What To Learn

- map regimes
- write explicit principles
- think in scenarios

## What Not To Copy

- do not let a macro framework replace asset-specific evidence

## Common Misreadings

- principles are not automatic truth
- macro frameworks can over-explain
- diversification is not a personal allocation recommendation
