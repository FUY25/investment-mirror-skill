# Jesse Livermore

## Source-Backed Biography

Jesse Livermore was a famous speculator whose life is often used as both a trading psychology case and a cautionary risk story.

## Investment Style

Speculative trading archetype centered on price action, timing, and crowd behavior, best used in this product as a warning lens.

## Notable Results And Track-Record Context

His public significance is as a historical trading figure and cautionary case about speculation, leverage, and psychology.

This profile intentionally avoids unsupported annualized-return claims, league-table rankings, and any implication that the master endorses a current security.

## What To Learn

- respect leverage and ruin risk
- do not confuse price action with business analysis
- define loss boundaries

## What Not To Copy

- do not romanticize speculation or ignore life-cycle and leverage risk

## Common Misreadings

- legendary trades do not make a risk process
- tape reading is not investment research
- cautionary cases are not avatars to emulate
