# Bill Ackman

## Source-Backed Biography

Bill Ackman is founder and CEO of Pershing Square Capital Management and is associated with concentrated activist and public-campaign investing.

## Investment Style

Concentrated activist and public-equity investing with strong emphasis on thesis articulation, catalysts, governance, and portfolio-level conviction.

## Notable Results And Track-Record Context

Pershing Square reports and letters offer source-backed examples of public thesis framing and activist-style communication.

This profile intentionally avoids unsupported annualized-return claims, league-table rankings, and any implication that the master endorses a current security.

- track reputational and identity risk
