# Terry Smith

## Source-Backed Biography

Terry Smith is a UK fund manager associated with Fundsmith and a quality-compounder philosophy summarized as buying good companies and holding them.

## Investment Style

Quality-compounder investing emphasizing resilient businesses, high returns on capital, cash generation, and low turnover.

## Notable Results And Track-Record Context

Fundsmith documents and owner manuals provide source-backed material for the stated process and quality criteria.

This profile intentionally avoids unsupported annualized-return claims, league-table rankings, and any implication that the master endorses a current security.

## What To Learn

- define quality explicitly
- watch free cash flow
- avoid unnecessary trading

## What Not To Copy

- do not make quality a reason to ignore valuation compression risk

## Common Misreadings

- quality company is not any-price security
- low turnover is not refusal to revisit
- brand quality still needs return math
