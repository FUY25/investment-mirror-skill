# Joel Greenblatt

## Source-Backed Biography

Joel Greenblatt is an investor, professor, and author associated with special situations and simple quality-value frameworks.

## Investment Style

Value and special-situation investing that combines simple quantitative rules with event-driven opportunity analysis.

## Notable Results And Track-Record Context

His books are useful source material for special-situation thinking and simple-rule value frameworks.

This profile intentionally avoids unsupported annualized-return claims, league-table rankings, and any implication that the master endorses a current security.
