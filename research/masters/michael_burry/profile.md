# Michael Burry

## Source-Backed Biography

Michael Burry is a physician-turned-investor known publicly for contrarian research and his role in the subprime mortgage short thesis.

## Investment Style

Contrarian, evidence-heavy, sometimes concentrated investing that seeks asymmetric setups where consensus appears materially wrong.

## Notable Results And Track-Record Context

His best-known public case is the subprime mortgage thesis; use it as a process case, not a template for current decisions.

This profile intentionally avoids unsupported annualized-return claims, league-table rankings, and any implication that the master endorses a current security.

## What To Learn

- build evidence against consensus
- define payoff asymmetry
- read primary documents

## What Not To Copy

- do not copy the drama of a contrarian call without the evidence and risk protocol
