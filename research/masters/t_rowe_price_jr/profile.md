# T. Rowe Price Jr.

## Source-Backed Biography

T. Rowe Price Jr. founded the investment firm bearing his name and is associated with growth-stock investing and long-term business lifecycle analysis.

## Investment Style

Long-term growth investing focused on companies with durable expansion potential and compounding earnings power.

## Notable Results And Track-Record Context

His significance is primarily as an early architect of growth-investing practice and the founder of T. Rowe Price.

This profile intentionally avoids unsupported annualized-return claims, league-table rankings, and any implication that the master endorses a current security.

## What To Learn

- map business lifecycle
- ask whether growth can endure
- connect growth to expectations

## What Not To Copy

- do not assume a growing company is attractive without asking what growth is already priced in

## Common Misreadings

- growth is not momentum by another name
- long-run durability still needs price expectations
- institutional history is not a stock thesis
